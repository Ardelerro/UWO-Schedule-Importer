Schedule Importer

Privacy & Data Usage Disclosure

PURPOSE What this extension does

Uwo Schedule Importer allows users to import their university course schedule from the UWO Draft My Schedule website into their Google Calendar. All actions are initiated directly by the user.

DATA Data we access

  • Website content: Course information such as course codes, meeting times, locations, and instructors displayed on the Draft My Schedule webpage.
  • Personally identifiable information: Schedule data may be user-specific, but student no names, emails, or student numbers are collected.
  • Authentication information: Google authentication is handled via Google OAuth. The extension does not collect or store passwords.

USE How data is used

The accessed data is processed locally within the browser and used only to create calendar events in the user’s Google Calendar at the user’s request. No data is stored or transmitted to any external servers beyond using google's official APIs.

SHARING Data sharing

Data is shared only with Google Calendar through official Google APIs for the purpose of creating calendar events. No data is sent to any other servers or third parties.

STORAGE Data retention

The extension does not permanently store user data. All processing is temporary and occurs during the import process.

We do not sell, track, or monetize user data.
